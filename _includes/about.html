About DIREQT2Heart


A DIRect QuanTitative assessment of Heart disease with magnetic resonance imaging. It is a project that Prompt diagnosis of coronary heart disease to prevent early mortality. It is coordenated by Teresa Correia from CCMAR within the Gambelas Campus of the University of Algarve (Faro, Portugal), This includes the development of rapid imaging, motion correction, deep learning-based reconstruction and quantification approaches for Magnetic Resonance Imaging in cardiology.
