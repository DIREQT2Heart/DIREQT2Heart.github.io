About DIREQT2Heart


A DIRect QuanTitative assessment of Heart disease with magnetic resonance imaging. It is a project that Prompt diagnosis of coronary heart disease to prevent early mortality. The consortium is led by Teresa Matias Correia from CCMAR and includes Rita Nunes from Associação do Instituto Superior Técnico para a Investigação e Desenvolvimento (ISR), Carlos Alberola-López from University of Valladolid and Borja Ibáñez from Centro Nacional de Investigaciones Cardiovasculares Carlos III (CNIC).
